Got Questions?

The Nitty-Gritty

Still scratching your head? We've got answers. Probably.

What's a mechanical engineer?

Someone who makes things go whizz, bang, and occasionally, 'oops'.

Can you build a robot?

Depends. Does it need to fetch snacks? Maybe.

What's your specialty?

Making complex systems behave. Like toddlers, but with more torque.

Do you do magic?

No, just really, really smart engineering. Close enough, right?

What about thermodynamics?

It's hot stuff, literally. We make heat work for us.

Can you fix my car?

Probably. But you might not like the bill. Or the lecture.
