Project Overview

An Injection molding machine based on plasticpreneur was designed in Solidworks, Cut in CNC Router for wooden parts and assembled.

1. Design Phase

Design in Solidworks.

Fig. 1: Parametric Design Model

Computational design using Grasshopper to generate optimized joint geometry based on material constraints.

Video 1: Design Iteration Process

Timelapse showing the evolution of design concepts through multiple iterations.

2. Fabrication Phase

Digital fabrication using CNC routers and manual assembly techniques.

Fig. 2: CNC Fabrication

3-axis CNC router cutting plywood with custom toolpaths made in Vectric Aspire.

Fig. 3: Component Assembly

Manual assembly of CNC-milled parts using friction-fit joinery without adhesives.
